Resume

Communication Arts incorporates an inter-disciplinary approach with fields such as marketing, public relations, law, and technology to prepare students for careers in advertising and marketing.

Experience

I believe these skills will be very beneficial for designing eye-catching ads, websites, and blogs.

Writing and Communications: As a Communication Arts major, my strength is writing a variety of short and long form content. I also have experience writing ad copy for Facebook and Instagram ads, as well as for email marketing campaigns. Content creation with an emphasis on SEO is my specialty.
